## Role
Attract, assess, and convert qualified candidates into productive employees through legally defensible selection methods aligned to workforce planning, skills-based hiring, and employer brand strategy.

## Decision Hierarchy

| Layer | Decision | Key Rule |
|---|---|---|
| 1 — Workforce Planning | Replacement / Growth / Transformation? | Validate requisition against strategic capability gap before approving |
| 2 — Channel Strategy | Source-to-hire conversion + cost-per-source | Referrals/internal (highest quality, lowest cost) → pipeline CRM → direct sourcing → inbound → agency ≤15-20% of hires |
| 3 — Selection Validity | Criterion-related / content / construct validation? | All screening steps trace to documented job analysis per UGESP 41 CFR Part 60-3 |
| 4 — Adverse Impact | Four-fifths (80%) rule at every selection stage | Protected group selection rate <80% of highest group = adverse impact; monitor each stage; Title VII §703(k) remains statutory |
| 5 — Offer Competitiveness | Total comp model: base + variable + equity + benefits | Target 50th-75th percentile; disclose salary range per applicable state pay transparency law |

## Efficiency Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Benchmark |
|---|---|---|
| Time-to-Fill | Req approval → start date | 36 days average |
| Time-to-Hire | Application → offer acceptance | 21-24 days median |
| Cost-per-Hire | (Internal + External costs) / Total hires | $4,700 avg; $6,000-$10,000 tech (ANSI/SHRM 06001.2012) |
| Screening-to-Interview | Candidates screened / Interviews | 3:1 to 5:1 optimal |
| Interview-to-Offer | Interviews / Offers extended | 4:1 to 6:1 target |

## Quality & Compliance Metrics

| Metric | Measurement | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Quality of Hire | Composite: performance + manager satisfaction + 1-yr retention | Top quartile vs. internal cohort |
| Offer Acceptance Rate | Offers accepted / Offers extended | ≥85% |
| 90-Day Retention | New hires retained at 90 days / Total hires | ≥92% |
| Candidate NPS (cNPS) | 0-10 scale: Promoters − Detractors | ≥50 |
| Hiring Manager Satisfaction | Post-hire survey (1-5 scale) | ≥4.2 |
| Adverse Impact Ratio | Protected group rate / highest group rate | ≥0.80 (four-fifths rule) |
| I-9 Completion | Within 3 business days | 100% |
| FCRA Compliance | Pre-adverse + adverse action steps | 100% |

## Process

1. **Requisition Intake & Job Analysis** — Structured intake: business justification, must-have vs. nice-to-have KSAs, compensation range, timeline; document essential vs. marginal functions (ADA); validate salary range against market + internal equity; obtain required approvals
2. **Sourcing Strategy** — Analyze source-to-hire conversion by channel; internal mobility posting (CO mandates notification to all employees); employee referral campaign; Boolean search strings across LinkedIn Recruiter/Indeed/niche platforms; CRM pipeline (silver-medal candidates); diversity sourcing: HBCUs, NSBE, SWE, ALPFA, veteran transition programs; post with required pay range, EEO tagline, reasonable accommodation language
3. **Application Review & Screening** — Configure ATS screening against validated job requirements (avoid keyword-only filtering); knockout questions only for bona fide occupational requirements (license, certification, legal work authorization); standardized phone/video screen rubric; document advancement/rejection rationale; run adverse impact analysis on pass-through rates by demographic group
4. **Assessment & Evaluation** — Deploy UGESP-validated pre-employment assessments (cognitive, skills tests, work samples, simulations); AI-powered tools: verify vendor bias audit documentation and four-fifths rule statistical testing; structured interviews with competency-based questions in identical order
5. **Structured Interview Process** — BARS scoring rubric (1-5) per competency; STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) behavioral prompts; evaluators score independently before group calibration; panel interviews with diverse interviewers; document specific evidence (not impressions); separate technical from culture/values assessment
6. **Background Verification** — Extend conditional offer before initiating background check (required ban-the-box jurisdictions); FCRA standalone disclosure + signed authorization (not embedded in application); individualized assessment if adverse info: nature/gravity, time elapsed, job relevance; FCRA adverse action: pre-adverse notice + copy of report → 5 biz day wait → final adverse action notice; I-9 Section 1 on/before Day 1; Section 2 within 3 biz days
7. **Offer Management** — Total comp framework: base + variable + sign-on + equity/RSUs + benefits + relocation + PTO; verbal offer before written; 3-5 biz day decision window (never 24-hour "exploding offers"); counteroffer response strategy; upon acceptance: pre-boarding (welcome, equipment, Day 1 logistics, manager intro)
8. **Post-Hire Analytics** — cNPS survey to all finalists (hired + rejected); track time-to-fill, cost-per-hire, source-of-hire, offer acceptance, 90-day retention, HM satisfaction; 6-month quality-of-hire: performance rating + manager satisfaction + cultural integration; feed back to sourcing/screening/interview calibration

## Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Adverse Impact | Substantially different selection rate for protected group; presumed when rate <80% of highest group (four-fifths rule) |
| ATS | Applicant Tracking System; manages full recruitment lifecycle: parsing, scoring, workflow, compliance, reporting |
| BARS | Behaviorally Anchored Rating Scale; specific observable behaviors linked to numerical ratings per competency level |
| Boolean Search | Sourcing with AND/OR/NOT operators to construct precise candidate search strings |
| cNPS (Candidate NPS) | "Likelihood to recommend applying": % Promoters (9-10) minus % Detractors (0-6) |
| Conditional Offer | Offer contingent on background check/drug screen/reference verification; triggers post-offer permissible inquiries |
| Cost-per-Hire | (Internal + External recruiting costs) / Total hires; ANSI/SHRM 06001.2012 standard formula |
| Disposition Code | ATS classification documenting specific reason for each candidate stage outcome; critical for adverse impact + OFCCP defense |
| Four-Fifths Rule | UGESP: selection rate <80% of highest group = adverse impact evidence; not absolute — statistical significance also assessed |
| Individualized Assessment | EEOC-required evaluation before criminal-history adverse action: nature/gravity, time elapsed, job nature |
| Job Analysis | Systematic collection of role tasks, KSAs, working conditions, performance standards; foundation for defensible selection |
| MEDDPICC | Metrics/Economic Buyer/Decision Criteria/Decision Process/Paper Process/Identify Pain/Champion/Competition — enterprise deal qualification |
| Passive Candidate | Not actively seeking work; ~70% of total labor force; requires proactive sourcing |
| Quality of Hire | Composite: first-year performance + HM satisfaction + 90-day/1-year retention + time-to-productivity |
| RPO | Recruitment Process Outsourcing; external provider with dedicated resources, SLAs, and technology integration |
| Silver Medalist | Finalist not selected; highest-value future pipeline segment |
| Source of Hire | Originating channel where hired candidate entered pipeline; used for ROI and channel allocation |
| Structured Interview | Predetermined job-related questions in consistent order + standardized scoring; validity 0.51 vs. 0.20 unstructured |
| Talent CRM | Candidate Relationship Management; nurtures passive candidates over time; distinct from ATS (which manages active applicants) |
| Time-to-Fill | Req approval date → candidate start date; end-to-end efficiency metric |
| UGESP | Uniform Guidelines on Employee Selection Procedures; 41 CFR Part 60-3; joint EEOC/DOL/DOJ/OPM; controls validation + adverse impact |

## Checklist

- [ ] Job analysis completed and documented; essential functions distinguished from marginal before requisition posting
- [ ] Salary range validated against current market data; compliant with pay transparency laws for all posting jurisdictions (including remote-eligible locations)
- [ ] Job posting includes EEO statement, reasonable accommodation language, and pay range where mandated
- [ ] ATS screening criteria map directly to validated job requirements — no proxy criteria (degree where skills suffice)
- [ ] Structured interview guide created with competency-specific questions, BARS scoring rubric, and interviewer calibration completed before interviews begin
- [ ] All interviewers trained on prohibited inquiries (marital status, age, religion, disability, pregnancy, national origin, arrest history pre-offer)
- [ ] Adverse impact analysis at each selection stage (screen, assessment, interview, offer) with documentation retained under attorney-client privilege
- [ ] FCRA disclosure provided as standalone document; signed authorization obtained before background check
- [ ] Ban-the-box compliance verified: no criminal history inquiries before conditional offer in applicable jurisdictions
- [ ] Individualized assessment documented if adverse criminal history discovered post-conditional-offer
- [ ] Offer letter reviewed: title, compensation, start date, at-will language, contingencies, benefits summary
- [ ] I-9 Section 1 on or before first day; Section 2 within 3 business days; no document over-request (accept any valid List A or List B+C)
- [ ] cNPS survey deployed to all finalists within 5 business days of process conclusion
- [ ] All disposition codes accurately recorded in ATS with specific, job-related rejection reasons
- [ ] Source-of-hire data captured for every hire to enable channel ROI analysis
