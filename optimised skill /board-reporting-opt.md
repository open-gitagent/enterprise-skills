## Role
Prepare and deliver decision-ready board financial packages that combine GAAP-compliant financials, forward-looking KPIs, and narrative under SEC/SOX/exchange compliance.

## Decision Framework

| Axis | Rule |
|------|------|
| Audience Calibration | Communicate at level of least financially sophisticated director; metrics needing >1 sentence to define belong in a glossary appendix |
| Signal-to-Noise | Board sees 15–25 KPIs max; include only if (a) confirms board-approved objective or (b) surfaces risk requiring board discussion |
| Narrative Before Numbers | Lead with 1–2 page exec summary: What happened? Why? What next? Financials are supporting evidence |
| Forward-Over-Backward Ratio | ≥40% of package must address future (forecasts, scenarios, risk outlook) |
| No-Surprises Doctrine | Material variances communicated to board chair/CEO individually 48–72 hours before distribution |

## Regulatory Requirements

| Requirement | Key Obligation |
|-------------|---------------|
| Reg S-K Item 303 (MD&A) | Discuss known material trends, demands, commitments, uncertainties affecting financial condition |
| Regulation FD | Prohibits selective disclosure of material nonpublic information |
| Regulation G | GAAP reconciliation required for all non-GAAP measures disclosed publicly |
| SOX §302 | CEO/CFO certify accuracy and ICFR effectiveness within 90 days of each report filing |
| SOX §404 | Annual ICFR assessment; external auditor attestation (accelerated/large accelerated filers) |
| SOX §906 | Criminal penalties: up to $5M fine, 20 years imprisonment for willful non-compliant certifications |
| NYSE §303A.07 | Audit committee reviews annual/quarterly financials, MD&A, earnings releases with management and external auditor |
| NASDAQ Rule 5605(c) | Audit committee reviews financials and disclosures; discusses reporting quality quarterly |
| PCAOB AS 2201 | Auditor evaluates entity-level controls including board/audit committee oversight |
| PCAOB AS 4105 | Quarterly review procedures; communicate significant deficiencies to audit committee |
| DGCL §141(e) | Directors entitled to rely in good faith on adequate and timely reports from officers |
| EU CSRD / ESRS | Large EU companies report ESG under double materiality; board oversight required |
| ISSB IFRS S1/S2 | Baseline sustainability and climate disclosure standards adopted by multiple jurisdictions from 2025 |
| California SB 253/261 | Scope 1–3 emissions + climate risk for companies >$1B revenue in CA; eff. 2026–2027 |

## Process

### Phase 1: Pre-Close Alignment (T-20 to T-15)
1. Confirm board meeting agenda with CEO, board chair, corporate secretary
2. Identify 2–3 strategic topics requiring financial framing
3. Align with IR on concurrent disclosure obligations (if public)
4. Review prior board meeting action items and open questions
5. Confirm KPI definitions unchanged; prepare bridge if any metric redefined

### Phase 2: Financial Close & Data Assembly (T-15 to T-8)
6. Execute month/quarter-end close; lock trial balance by workday 3–4
7. Complete balance sheet reconciliations and variance analyses
8. Run P&L, balance sheet, cash flow, and segment-level reporting
9. Controller signs off; FP&A aligns on messaging
10. Pull non-financial KPIs from operational systems

### Phase 3: Package Construction (T-8 to T-5)
11. Draft executive summary: 1–2 pages, What/Why/What Next
12. Build KPI dashboard — single page, traffic-light indicators, trend lines over 4–6 periods
13. Prepare financial statements with period-over-period and budget-to-actual comparisons
14. Write variance commentary: material items (>5% or >$X threshold), root cause, remediation
15. Develop forward-looking section: updated forecast, scenario analysis, key assumptions
16. Include risk register updates, liquidity/covenant compliance, capital allocation status
17. Append ESG/sustainability metrics if required by governance framework
18. Build appendix with detailed backup for directors who want deeper detail

### Phase 4: Review & QA (T-5 to T-3)
19. CFO reviews for narrative coherence, data accuracy, strategic alignment
20. Cross-check every number in deck against source financial statements
21. Legal/compliance reviews for Reg FD and forward-looking statement safe harbors
22. CEO reviews and approves

### Phase 5: Distribution & Pre-Meeting (T-3 to T-0)
23. Distribute via secure board portal 7–14 days before meeting (NACD/Diligent best practice); minimum 5 business days
24. CFO pre-reads with audit committee chair and board chair for sensitive items
25. Pre-brief directors with specific concerns; prepare talking points and Q&A

### Phase 6: Post-Meeting (T+1 to T+5)
26. Document board decisions, information requests, action items
27. Archive final package with audit trail
28. Incorporate feedback into process improvement log

## Evaluation Criteria

| Dimension | Weight | Scoring |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Data Accuracy | 25% | Zero tolerance: any number not reconciling to audited financials = fail |
| Narrative Quality | 20% | Executive summary enables director to grasp trajectory in <3 minutes |
| Strategic Relevance | 20% | ≥40% of content addresses forward-looking strategy, risks, opportunities |
| Timeliness | 10% | ≥7 days before meeting = full; 5–6 days = partial; <5 days = fail |
| Visual Clarity | 10% | Off-track KPIs identifiable within 10 seconds of viewing dashboard |
| Compliance Completeness | 10% | All required disclosures, committee content, regulatory references present |
| Actionability | 5% | Each section concludes with clear implications and specific asks |

## Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| Board Package | Complete set of materials distributed to directors; exec summary, financials, KPI dashboard, risk register, committee materials |
| Variance Analysis | Quantification of differences between actuals and budget/forecast/prior year; material = >5% or fixed dollar threshold |
| KPI Dashboard | Single-page visual of 15–25 KPIs with traffic-light indicators, trend lines, benchmarks |
| Executive Summary | 1–2 page narrative answering What/Why/What Next; most important page in the package |
| Rolling Forecast | Continuously updated 12–18 month projection; reforecasted monthly or quarterly |
| MD&A | Management Discussion & Analysis; narrative of financial condition per Reg S-K Item 303 |
| Non-GAAP Measures | Metrics excluding GAAP items (e.g., Adjusted EBITDA); require Reg G reconciliation |
| Traffic-Light (RAG) | Red/Amber/Green coding on KPIs; thresholds must be calibrated to avoid all-green sandbagging |
| Covenant Compliance | Adherence to debt ratio requirements; reported with headroom analysis showing distance to threshold |
| Going Concern | Management evaluation per ASC 205-40 of ability to continue operations 12 months from issuance date |
| ICFR | Internal Controls Over Financial Reporting; subject to SOX §404 assessment |
| Regulation FD | Prohibits selective disclosure of material nonpublic information to analysts/institutions without simultaneous public disclosure |
| Consent Agenda | Routine board items grouped for single-motion approval to free time for strategic discussion |
| Board Portal | Secure digital platform (Diligent, Nasdaq Boardvantage, OnBoard) for distributing and archiving board materials |
| Bridge Analysis | Visual walkthrough showing how a metric moved from one state to another by component |
| Segment Reporting | Financial results by operating segment per ASC 280/IFRS 8; essential for capital allocation decisions |
| Earnings Quality | Degree to which reported earnings derive from recurring cash-generating operations vs. one-time items |
| Safe Harbor Statement | Qualifying language for forward-looking statements per Private Securities Litigation Reform Act 1995 |
| Double Materiality | EU CSRD framework: report both financial impact of sustainability issues AND company's impact on people/environment |
| North Star Metric | Single most critical KPI at dashboard top anchoring board evaluation before drilling into supporting metrics |

## Checklist

- [ ] Every number in deck ties to a reconcilable source in audited/reviewed financials
- [ ] Executive summary fits 1–2 pages; answers What/Why/What Next; readable in <3 minutes
- [ ] KPI dashboard fits single page; traffic-light thresholds recalibrated within last 12 months
- [ ] Variance commentary addresses all items exceeding materiality threshold with root cause + remediation
- [ ] Forward-looking content comprises ≥40% of total package
- [ ] Non-GAAP measures include GAAP reconciliation and consistent definitions across periods
- [ ] Segment-level financial performance disclosed alongside consolidated results
- [ ] Covenant compliance includes headroom analysis — not just binary in/out status
- [ ] Package distributed via secure board portal ≥7 business days before meeting; access acknowledgments tracked
- [ ] Legal/compliance reviewed forward-looking statements for safe harbor and Reg FD implications
- [ ] Audit committee materials are distinct and more detailed than general board materials per NYSE §303A.07 / NASDAQ 5605(c)
- [ ] Format, KPI definitions, section structure consistent with prior quarter; changes bridged and explained
- [ ] ESG/sustainability metrics disclose methodology, baseline, and boundary definitions
- [ ] Cash flow statement and liquidity analysis receive narrative treatment equal to P&L
- [ ] Prior board meeting action items addressed with status updates
